Human Stew

Descripiton:

A stew made from human flesh and organs.

Ingredients:

  • 100g flesh
  • 50g human heart
  • 1L blood
  • 1m of intestines
  • 1-2 eyes

Steps:

  1. Put the blood till' is boiling
  2. Add the flesh and wait 10 minutes
  3. Add the heart and intestines
  4. Wait 30 minutes and is good for serving
  5. For each serve put an eyeball
