Heart Cookies

Descripiton:

Heart cookies made from human heart.

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 human hearts
  • 300ml of blood
  • 200g flour

Steps:

  1. Mix the flour with the blood and let it 30 minutes in the fridge
  2. Clear the hearts on the inside
  3. Fill them with the mixture
  4. Put it in the stove for one hour
