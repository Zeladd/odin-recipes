Eyeball Pudding

Descripiton:

An eyeball filled with blood pudding.

Ingredients:

  • 1 eyeball
  • 100ml blood

Steps:

  1. Put the blood in a small bowl for 30 minutes in the fridge
  2. Cut the eyeball in half
  3. Clean the inside
  4. Fill the eyeball with blood and put it together
  5. Put in the fridge for another 30 minutes
